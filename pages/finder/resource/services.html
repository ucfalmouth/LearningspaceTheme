  • ASK (Academic Skills)

    The ASK: Academic Skills service provides one­to­one advice on the skills you will need for academic study: essay writing skills, study skills needed for effective time management and presentations. Research skills, numeracy and English language support for students whose first language is not English.

  • Creative Futures Hub (Employability)

    Creative Futures Hub is your online one­stop­shop for all events, job vacancies, software training, work placements and booking appointments with Careers and Placements staff.

  • Dyslexia Skills

    Dyslexia Skills provides individual advice and guidance for students who have been identified as having dyslexia. Our team can enable you to maximise the capabilities that working with dyslexia can bring. Skills include mind mapping, effective note taking and tackling your reading list.

  • Finance Services

    Finance Services supports all the financial needs of staff and students at Falmouth University. From bursaries, scholarships and student funding to staff Agresso training, Finance Services will address your finance related queries.

  • FXU

    FXU is the combined students’ union for Falmouth University and the University of Exeter in Cornwall. We ensure that all students of both universities have the best possible experience while studying in Cornwall. Support, advice, representation, sports, societies and entertainments are offered throughout the year.

  • International Office

    Small enough to be welcoming, yet laterally focused enough to encourage creative enterprise, the International Office has built on over 100 years of experience in welcoming international students to our diverse community. Voted the number one institution in the world for international student experience, we are fully committed to delivering a world class education.

  • IT Services

    The IT Service Desk provides IT help and information for students and staff at Penryn and Falmouth campus. It is the first point of contact for any queries or issues relating to University IT equipment and systems. These include: online resources, email, IT accounts, printing, WiFi (FXNET) and other services. A laptop clinic service is available if you would like advice or help with personal IT equipment, and the IT Service Desk can loan out laptops for a period of one week.

  • Library and Information Services

    Library and Information Services offer a wide range of resources, including books, journals, newspapers, DVD's, CDs, a music collection, images, and dissertations. Online resources include e­books, e­journals and electronic databases. These resources can be used both on and off campus, and are intended for coursework and assignments. All Library staff are here to assist you and the Academic Liaison Librarian responsible for your course, offers one­to­one appointments for in­depth subject help.

  • Quality Office & Results Release

    The Quality and Standards Team is responsible for ensuring the quality and enhancement of the student experience, through the development and delivery of appropriate policies and frameworks. They are responsible for the dissemination of award results, across the university.

  • Student Support

    The Student Support Services team are here to help and guide you through your time at university, by providing access to a range of services and information. We provide one­to­one advice and guidance to enable every student to make the most of their university experience, despite any difficulties they may face. We are here to support all students, whether you require information about childcare, access to learning applications, or to simply offer a friendly ear when needed. Whatever you may be facing, we are here to assist and help you through it.

  • The Compass

    Whether you have questions that are big or small, academic or otherwise, The Compass is here to help and point you in the right direction (hence the name). Our friendly team of advisors, will help you find the answers to the most common issues, and if needed, will be able to refer you to the right team for more in­depth advice.
